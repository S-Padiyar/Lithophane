About Litho3D

Litho3D transforms your cherished memories into stunning 3D-printed lithophanes, blending cutting-edge technology with artisan craftsmanship. Our mission is to create personalized keepsakes that capture the essence of your special moments, perfect for gifting or decorating your home.

High-Res Printing

Using advanced 3D printers with 0.1mm layer precision, we achieve crisp details and smooth gradients, ensuring your images come to life with exceptional clarity and depth.

Eco Conscious

We use biodegradable PLA filament and partner with reforestation initiatives to plant a tree for every order, contributing to a sustainable future for our planet.

Artisan Finish

Each lithophane is meticulously hand-sanded, polished, and quality-checked by our skilled artisans to deliver a flawless, premium product that feels as good as it looks.

Our Process

Turn your favorite photo into a glowing keepsake in four seamless steps. Our user-friendly process combines intuitive tools with expert craftsmanship to ensure a hassle-free experience from start to finish.

1

Upload Photo

Easily drag and drop your high-contrast JPG or PNG. Our system supports various image formats and provides instant feedback to ensure optimal results.

2

Preview & Crop

Use our real-time editor to reposition, resize, or apply filters to your image, allowing you to perfect the composition before printing begins.

3

Print & Finish

Our team prints your design with precision, then hand-finishes each piece with care, polishing and packaging it securely for delivery to your door.

4

Enjoy & Share

Display your lithophane on an LED base or in a window to reveal its magical glow, perfect for sharing with loved ones or cherishing forever.
